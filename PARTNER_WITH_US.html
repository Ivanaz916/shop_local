Shop Local Arlington is a free, community-driven platform built to drive foot traffic back to the independent shops lining Mass Ave — with immersive 360° tours, AI-powered search, and page-view insights, at absolutely no cost to you.

Benefits

WHY PARTNER WITH US?

Six reasons to list your shop today.

Increase Foot Traffic

Your shop gets discovered by local residents and visitors browsing Mass Ave online — turning digital interest into people through your door.

Immersive 360° Tours

Customers can step inside your shop before they arrive, building the familiarity and confidence that encourages them to visit in person.

AI-Powered Search

Site visitors search for items across every listed shop at once. When your inventory is on the platform, it shows up in their results.

Page-View Metrics

See how many people viewed your shop page and what products they searched for — simple, actionable insight with no technical setup required.

Community Board

Neighbors post "looking for" requests on the site. A well-stocked listing means your shop comes up when someone is already looking to buy.

Zero Cost

The platform is free and community-driven. No subscription, no commission, no hidden fees — now or in the future.

B

360° Interior Panoramas

Immersive interior photos let site visitors step inside your shop before they arrive. Multiple panoramas are welcome — the goal is to cover as much of your store as possible.

Free apps to capture panoramas

AppPlatformHow
Google Maps (photosphere)AndroidMaps → Contribute → Camera → Photo Sphere (feature availability varies by app version)
Panorama 360 CameraiOS / AndroidFree app, exports equirectangular JPEGs — recommended for reliable high-res output
Google Street View APKAndroidVersion 2.0.0.303 retains photosphere capture; search APK Mirror for the installer

Tips for a quality result

  • Stand in the center of each room or area
  • Hold the phone at chest height
  • Follow the on-screen guide dots slowly and steadily
  • Avoid moving objects — people, fans, open doors
  • Target resolution: 3840 × 1920 px (2:1 aspect ratio) — lower-resolution shots are still usable; we will advise if quality is too low to publish
  • Take multiple shots and we will select the best stitch
C

Shop Owner Profile (optional)

Putting a face to the shop strengthens the personal connection that makes local retail special. If you are comfortable sharing:

  • A photo and a short bio about you and your shop's story
  • Contact details — we may add a direct-messaging option so site visitors can reach you with questions, mirroring the experience of walking in and chatting in person

Sharing contact information is entirely voluntary but encouraged — it builds community trust and keeps the shopping experience personal.

D

Update Cadence

By default we schedule a quarterly check-in to refresh your inventory, photos, and shop details. If you prefer a different rhythm — more frequent, less frequent, or updates as-needed — just let us know.
